A Quick And Easy Chocolate Mug Cake!

By Ira


Here's a simple recipe for a decadent chocolate mug cake that you can whip up in minutes:

    Ingredients:
  • Instructions:3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powde
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of milk

Instructions:

Combine dry ingredients: In a mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.Add wet ingredients: Stir in the milk, oil, and vanilla extract until just combined.Microwave: Microwave on high for 60-90 seconds, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.Serve: Let cool slightly before serving. Top with whipped cream, ice cream, or a drizzle of chocolate sauce.Enjoy your quick and delicious treat!
For a healthier twist

For a healthier twist: Consider using whole wheat flour, a natural sweetener like honey or maple.
